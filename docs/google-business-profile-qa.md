## Question 10: Rock Chip Repair

**Q:** Can you repair small rock chips or do I need full replacement?

**A:** Many rock chips CAN be repaired if they're:
- Smaller than a quarter (about 1 inch diameter)
- Not directly in the driver's line of sight
- Not at the edge of the windshield
- Not cracked through multiple layers

Rock chip repairs take only 20-30 minutes and are often covered 100% by insurance with no deductible. We'll inspect your chip for free and advise whether repair or replacement is the safest option. Call (720) 918-7465 or text us a photo of the damage for a free assessment!
