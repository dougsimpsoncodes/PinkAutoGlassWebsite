# Google Posts Templates - Weekly Content

Post updates to your Google Business Profile every 7-10 days to keep your profile active and engage customers.

**How to Post:**
1. Log into Google Business Profile
2. Click "Add update" or "Create post"
3. Choose post type (What's new, Event, Offer, or Product)
4. Copy/paste template below
5. Add a photo if available
6. Add call-to-action button ("Call now", "Learn more", "Book")
7. Publish!
