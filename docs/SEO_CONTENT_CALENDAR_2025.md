## Ongoing Monthly Tasks

### Every Week:
1. **Google Business Posts:**
   - Monday: Service spotlight (rotate services)
   - Thursday: Customer testimonial or tip
   - Include booking CTA in every post

2. **Local Citations:**
   - Add to 2-3 new directories per week
   - Ensure NAP consistency across all listings

3. **Review Management:**
   - Respond to all Google reviews within 24 hours
   - Request reviews from satisfied customers

### Every 2 Weeks:
1. **Internal Linking Audit:**
   - Add internal links from new blog posts to older posts
   - Update cornerstone content with links to new pages

2. **Competitor Analysis:**
   - Check competitor rankings for target keywords
   - Identify new keyword opportunities

### Monthly:
1. **Performance Review:**
   - Track clicks, impressions, CTR, position (Search Console)
   - Identify top gaining/losing pages
   - Adjust strategy based on data

2. **Content Refresh:**
   - Update 2-3 older blog posts with new information
   - Add FAQ schema to pages that don't have it
   - Refresh meta descriptions for underperforming pages

3. **Link Building Outreach:**
   - Contact 10 relevant websites for backlinks
   - Submit guest post pitches
   - Engage with industry publications
