## 📤 HOW TO UPLOAD TO GOOGLE ADS

### Method 1: Bulk Upload via CSV (Recommended)

**Step-by-Step:**

1. **Log into Google Ads**
   - Go to https://ads.google.com
   - Select your account (Pink Auto Glass)

2. **Navigate to Keywords Section**
   - Click "Keywords and Targeting" in left menu
   - Click "Search keywords"
   - Click "Negative keywords" tab at top

3. **Import CSV File**
   - Click the "+" button
   - Select "Upload keywords"
   - Choose "Select file"
   - Upload `negative-keywords-high-priority-updated.csv` first
   - Map columns:
     - Column 1 (Negative Keyword) → Keyword
     - Column 2 (Match Type) → Match type
   - Skip columns 3 & 4 (Reason, Data Source)
   - Click "Apply"

4. **Verify Upload**
   - Check that keywords appear in your negative keyword list
   - Verify match types are correct (Exact vs Phrase)

5. **Repeat for Comprehensive List**
   - After 2-3 days, upload `negative-keywords-upload-updated.csv`
   - Follow same steps

## 🛑 COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

### ❌ Don't Add These As Negatives:
- **aurora** - You serve Aurora (within Denver 30mi)
- **denver** - Your main market
- **colorado springs** - You serve this area
- **fort collins** - You serve this area
- **rear windshield** - You DO this service
- **back glass** - You DO this service
- **mobile windshield** - This is your service!

### ✅ DO Add These As Negatives:
- **Competitor brand names** (they're looking for someone else)
- **Cities >30mi from Denver** (waste of money)
- **Cities >10mi from Fort Collins/COS** (out of service area)
- **Informational "how much" queries** (not ready to buy)
- **DIY product terms** (want to do it themselves)

## 🔧 TROUBLESHOOTING

### "CSV won't upload"
- **Solution:** Check file format is UTF-8 CSV
- Try opening in Excel, save as "CSV UTF-8"
- Remove any special characters from keywords

### "Too many errors during upload"
- **Solution:** Upload in smaller batches
- Upload high-priority list first (33 keywords)
- Then upload comprehensive list in 50-keyword chunks

### "Don't see negative keywords taking effect"
- **Solution:** Wait 24-48 hours for Google to process
- Clear browser cache
- Check keywords are added at campaign level, not account level

### "Still seeing competitor brand impressions"
- **Solution:** Add more misspellings
- Example: safelite, safelight, safe lite, safe light
- Use "close variant" matching
