## 🎯 PRIORITY 1: Add "Cheapest" Keywords (30 minutes)

Your price-focused searches converted at **100% with CPAs of $15-27** vs your average $59. This is your biggest opportunity.

### Implementation Steps

**Step 1: Create New Ad Group (Optional but Recommended)**
1. Go to Google Ads → Campaigns
2. Select your search campaign
3. Click "+ AD GROUP"
4. Name it: "Price Shoppers - High Intent"

**Step 2: Add These Exact Match Keywords**

Copy/paste these into your keyword list:

```
[cheapest windshield near me]
[cheapest windshield replacement near me]
[cheapest auto glass near me]
[cheapest windshield replacement denver]
[cheapest auto glass replacement near me]
[cheapest mobile windshield replacement near me]
```

**Step 3: Set Bids**
- Start bids at: **$25-30** for each keyword
- These will likely have Quality Score 7-9 (good relevance)

**Step 4: Monitor Daily for First Week**
- Check impressions (should see 5-15/day)
- Check conversion rate (target: 50%+)
- Raise bids by $3-5 if not getting enough impressions

### Expected Results
- **Week 1:** 3-5 additional conversions at $18-25 CPA
- **Month 1:** 12-20 conversions from these keywords alone
- **Savings:** $30-40 per conversion vs current average

## ⚠️ IMPORTANT NOTES

### Don't Forget
1. **Mobile optimization is critical** - 60%+ of searches are mobile
2. **Call tracking** - Make sure phone clicks are tracked as conversions
3. **Landing page speed** - Every 1 second delay = 10% fewer conversions
4. **Competitor monitoring** - Check if they're bidding on your brand name

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- ❌ Don't set bids too low on high-intent keywords (you'll miss impressions)
- ❌ Don't forget to update landing pages when you update ads
- ❌ Don't pause winning keywords just because CPA is higher than average
- ❌ Don't make multiple changes at once (you won't know what worked)

### When to Scale Budget
Only increase budget when:
- ✅ CPA is below $50
- ✅ Conversion rate is above 18%
- ✅ You're hitting daily budget limits (losing impression share)
- ✅ You have capacity to handle more jobs

Start with +20% budget increase, monitor for 1 week, then increase another 20% if performance holds.
