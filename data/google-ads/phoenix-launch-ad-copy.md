# Phoenix Google Search Launch Ad Copy

Basis
- Mirrors what Denver taught us: replacement is the primary demand engine.
- Insurance supports replacement conversion but does not need its own standalone campaign at launch.
- Launch structure assumed: one Search campaign with two ad groups:
  - Core Replacement
  - Insurance / Zero-Deductible Replacement

Launch constraints
- Geo launch: Phoenix, Scottsdale, Mesa, Tempe
- No brand campaign
- No repair ad spend
- No ADAS ad spend
- Match types: exact + phrase only

## Notes from Denver learnings
- Keep replacement intent as the primary conversion path.
- Use insurance as a conversion booster, not a separate budget silo.
- Avoid repair-led copy and avoid generic broad traffic language.
