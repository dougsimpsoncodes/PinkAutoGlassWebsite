## How to Upload to Google Ads

### Method 1: Campaign-Level Negative Keywords
1. Go to Google Ads → Campaigns
2. Select your campaign
3. Click "Keywords" → "Negative keywords"
4. Click the blue "+" button
5. Select "Add negative keywords"
6. Copy/paste from CSV or upload file
7. Choose match types as specified

### Method 2: Negative Keyword List (Recommended)
1. Go to Tools & Settings → Negative keyword lists
2. Click "+" to create new list
3. Name it "Pink Auto Glass - Competitor Brands" (or by category)
4. Upload the CSV file
5. Apply list to all relevant campaigns
