## RECOMMENDATIONS

### Immediate Actions (High Impact)

1. **Add Competitor Brand Names** (Est. Savings: $150-200/month)
   - All major competitors like Safelite, Jiffy, Tavos, Auto Glass Now
   - Local competitors with high impressions

2. **Exclude Out-of-Service-Area Locations** (Est. Savings: $100-150/month)
   - Wyoming cities (Cheyenne, Laramie, Gillette)
   - Out-of-state cities
   - Colorado cities outside service radius

3. **Block Informational Queries** (Est. Savings: $30-50/month)
   - "how much", "how to", "cost", "estimate"
   - These rarely convert

### Medium Priority Actions

4. **Add Wrong Service Types** (Est. Savings: $20-40/month)
   - "mobile mechanics", "automotive", "semi truck"

5. **Consider Generic Terms** (Use Caution)
   - Some generic terms like "windshield replacement" generated conversions
   - Use phrase match instead of broad match
   - Monitor carefully

### Low Priority / Optional

6. **Price Shopping Terms**
   - Some "cheapest" terms actually converted
   - Consider keeping but monitor CPA
   - If CPA > $50, add to negative list

7. **Specific Vehicle Models**
   - Most are legitimate service requests
   - Only exclude specialty vehicles you can't service (Tesla, exotic cars)
