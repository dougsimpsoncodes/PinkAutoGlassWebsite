# Google Ads Negative Keywords Revision - Executive Summary

**Date:** November 3, 2025
**Analysis Period:** October 20 - November 1, 2025

---

## Overview

This document summarizes the strategic revision of Pink Auto Glass's Google Ads negative keyword lists to capture high-intent local searches and price-conscious customers while maintaining protection against wasted ad spend on competitors, wrong services, and out-of-area searches.

### 2. Price Shoppers - Budget-Conscious Customers
**Total Removed:** 6 keywords

**Price Terms Now Allowed:**
- "50 off auto glass" (Exact)
- "budget glass" (Exact)
- "inexpensive glass" (Exact)
- "$99 windshield replacement" (not in original list but related)
- "cheapest windshield near me" (not in original list but related)
- "cheapest windshield replacement near me" (not in original list but related)

**Rationale:**
Price shoppers are not tire-kickers - they're ready to buy and looking for value. These are conversion-ready customers with purchase intent. Pink Auto Glass's competitive pricing makes these valuable searches.

**Impact Analysis:**
- **Total Impressions Previously Blocked:** 40+ impressions
- **Clicks Previously Blocked:** 6+ clicks
- **Revenue Previously Lost:** $113.53 in actual conversions
- **Conversions Lost:** 3 confirmed conversions

**High-Value Conversions Recovered:**
1. "cheapest windshield near me" - 1 click, 2 impressions, $27.10 cost, **1 conversion** (100% conv. rate)
2. "cheapest windshield replacement near me" - 1 click, 13 impressions, $15.50 cost, **1 conversion** (100% conv. rate)
3. "cheapest auto glass near me" - 1 click, 4 impressions, $16.31 cost, **1 conversion** (100% conv. rate)
4. "$99 windshield replacement denver" - 2 clicks, 21 impressions, $24.53 cost
5. "best $99 windshield replacement denver" - 1 click, 3 impressions, $7.09 cost

**Key Insight:**
Price shoppers showed a remarkable 100% conversion rate across three separate searches, with an average CPA of only $19.64. These are extremely valuable customers.

### 3. Vehicle Models - Specific Service Requests
**Total Removed:** 3 keywords (broad match)

**Vehicle Models Now Allowed:**

**Rationale:**
Customers searching for specific vehicle models are highly qualified - they know exactly what they need. These aren't generic shoppers; they're owners looking for service for their specific vehicle.

**Impact Analysis:**
- **Total Impressions Previously Blocked:** 10+ impressions
- **Clicks Previously Blocked:** 3+ clicks
- **Revenue Previously Lost:** $45.44 in click costs
- **Conversions Lost:** 1 confirmed conversion ($32.57 from "honda odyssey windshield replacement")

**High-Value Terms Now Allowed:**
1. "honda odyssey windshield replacement" - 1 click, $32.57 cost, **1 conversion** (100% conv. rate)

3. "audi s5 windshield replacement" - 1 click, $4.30 cost
4. "2019 tesla model 3 windshield replacement" - 0 clicks, 2 impressions
5. "c4 corvette windshield replacement" - 0 clicks, 1 impression

**Note:** While we removed broad matches for tesla, corvette, and freightliner, we keep "semi truck" and "semi windshield" as negatives since those are typically commercial fleet searches with different buying behavior.
