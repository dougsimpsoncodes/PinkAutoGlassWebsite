1. Homepage Priority:
The homepage needs the most work. Should we focus on:
  • Visual design overhaul (hero section, imagery, branding)
  • Content strategy (value props, trust signals, social proof)
  • User flow optimization (clearer path to booking)
2. Missing Pages Strategy:
The navigation references 4 pages that don't exist. Should we:
  • Create placeholder pages to prevent 404 errors
  • Remove navigation items until pages are built
  • Build out these pages before polishing existing ones
3. Booking Flow Polish:
The booking system works but could be enhanced:
  • Visual design improvements (better styling, animations)
  • UX enhancements (progress saving, back button, field validation)
  • Mobile optimization (touch targets, keyboard handling)
4. Brand Identity:
Pink Auto Glass needs stronger branding:
  • Color palette refinement (current pink theme, but needs depth)
  • Logo design (currently text-based placeholder)
  • Typography hierarchy and consistency
  • Photography/imagery (service photos, before/after, team)
5. Mobile Experience:
How important is mobile-first design?
  • Most users likely book on mobile
  • Touch-friendly interface critical for forms
  • Phone number accessibility on mobile
6. Content Needs:
What content do we need to create/gather?
  • Service photos (windshield repair process, before/after)
  • Company information (about, team, certifications)
  • Customer testimonials and reviews
  • Service area maps and coverage details
