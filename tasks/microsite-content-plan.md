## STRATEGIC OVERVIEW

### Important SEO Risk Warning

Before diving in, a critical note on execution: Google's 2024-2026 guidance increasingly penalizes "satellite site" networks that exist solely to funnel traffic to a single business. The key to making these microsites work without triggering a manual penalty is:

1. **Each site must provide genuinely unique, standalone value** -- a visitor should find the site useful even if they never click through to Pink Auto Glass
2. **Cross-linking must be editorial in nature** -- not sitewide footer links from all 11 domains pointing to pinkautoglass.com
3. **Each site needs its own identity** -- different design, different voice, different value proposition
4. **Never use identical content across any two sites** -- including boilerplate footer text, about sections, or FAQ answers
5. **Each site should earn its own backlinks** through genuinely useful tools and content

The domains below are organized from highest-impact (build first) to lowest-impact (build last).
