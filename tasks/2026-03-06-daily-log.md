## RingCentral SMS Outage — Diagnosed and Fixed

### Symptom
Doug started receiving customer texts in RingCentral that he'd never seen before. Dan reported he wasn't seeing the same texts. Investigation revealed all outbound SMS had been broken since ~March 5.

### Root Cause
The **Fax/SMS recipient** for the main company number (+17209187465) in RingCentral's Auto-Receptionist settings was assigned to Doug's extension (103). When the `RINGCENTRAL_JWT_TOKEN` was regenerated on ~March 5 for Dan's extension (101), Dan's extension didn't have `SmsSender` capability on the business number — only `CallerId`. RingCentral only allows ONE extension to send/receive SMS from the main company number at a time.

Every outbound SMS from the website (admin forwarding, auto-replies, drip messages, review requests) failed with: **"Phone number doesn't belong to extension"**

### What Was Broken (March 5-6)
- Admin forwarding to Dan/Kody/Doug — not delivered
- Auto-replies to customers — not sent (970-404-0017 customer got no response)
- Review request SMS — all 5 scheduled for March 6 failed ("Max retries exceeded")
- Drip follow-up SMS — all failed
- Email sending was unaffected (uses Resend, not RingCentral)

### Fix Applied
1. **RingCentral Admin Portal** > Phone System > Auto-Receptionist > General Settings > Call Handling > Call/Fax/SMS Recipients > changed **Fax/SMS recipient** from Doug Simpson (Ext 103) to **Dan Shikiar (Ext 101)**
2. Redeployed to production to clear stale RC auth cache
3. Verified: production SMS sent successfully from business number +17209187465
