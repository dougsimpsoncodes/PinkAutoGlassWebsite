# Auto-Quoter — Site-Wide Integration & Conversion Plan

Date: 2026-05-29 · Source: multi-agent workflow (5 surface surveys+designs, 5 adversarial review lenses, synthesis). Status: PROPOSED — awaiting Doug. Phase 1 = go-live.

## Executive summary
Pink has two funnels that never meet: a price-first quoter (AutomatedQuoteForm) on exactly 2 pages with 0 inbound links, and a no-price 3-step /book flow that 97 site CTAs feed. The strategy is to resolve this WITHOUT a 97-CTA big-bang and WITHOUT rebuilding the working /book destination. Phase 1 ships exactly what is on staging (homepage quoter migration, new pricing, brand-aware ADAS, and the YMM->plate/VIN nudge inside AutomatedQuoteForm) so Doug gets the quoter live now. Phase 2 then gives every page a one-tap path to a real price via two centralized, additive changes — a site-wide Header 'See your price' link and a pathname-derived prefill on the two CTA chokepoints (CTAButtons + AboveFoldCTA) — plus making quote_form measurable per surface/market. The contested 'embed the quoter on top of /book' and 'embed on every SEO content page' ideas are deliberately CUT as over-builds that manufacture a double-count problem and add JS weight for little gain; the price experience reaches the long tail through links and the working in-place PricedHero booking, not through 140 page edits or a stacked-form rebuild. Net: quoter live immediately, every page reaches a price, both ad-conversion events stay distinct and intact, and zero destructive routing/form surgery in scope.

## Funnel decision
COEXIST BY ROLE — do NOT merge the two funnels and do NOT embed the quoter on top of /book. The quoter (quote_form) is the price-first entry layer; its own PricedHero embeds QuoteBookingForm so a priced visitor books in place and NEVER needs /book. The /book BookingFlow (booking_form) stays the universal closer for the long tail and the 97 existing CTAs — left structurally untouched. The 97-vs-0 imbalance is fixed by ADDING inbound paths to the quoter, not by rerouting 97 CTAs or rebuilding /book: (1) a site-wide Header 'See your price' link gives all 263 routes a one-tap price entrypoint from a single file; (2) the YMM-miss nudge inside AutomatedQuoteForm recovers the dead-end YMM path into plate/VIN live prices. The contested /book full-embed is REJECTED: stacking the 648-line quoter above the 3-step BookingFlow puts two booking endpoints on the highest-traffic destination, manufactures a priced-then-booked double-fire that then forces tracking surgery, and is the single biggest over-build flagged by 3 of 5 reviewers. Both Google/Microsoft Ads events stay distinct exactly as today (quote_form for priced quotes, booking_form for /book) — because the funnels stay on separate pages, no dedup re-key or send_to label split is required in scope; we only VERIFY both events are counted goals. Long-tail deep-link prefill targets /book's EXISTING ?location=&service= prefill (booking_form) as an honest typing-reduction lever and is correctly labeled as such, never as a price promise.

## Risks & mitigations
- **Risk:** Embedding the quoter on top of /book would put two booking endpoints (/api/quote/book via QuoteBookingForm + /api/booking/submit via BookingFlow) on the highest-traffic destination, manufacture a priced-then-booked double-fire, force tracking surgery, and strand 100% of booking traffic on any regression.
  - **Mitigation:** CUT the /book full-embed from scope entirely. Reach price-first via the additive Header link + the in-place PricedHero booking the quoter already has. /book stays the untouched no-price closer; both ad events stay distinct with no dedup re-key or send_to split needed.
- **Risk:** A new quote_form path on a live paid page reads as a conversion drop and triggers a spend cut to working pages (the 'added a funnel, looked like a regression' trap).
  - **Mitigation:** Hard pre-ship gate: confirm quote_form + booking_form are counted conversions in BOTH Ads accounts and annotate the change date before each paid-surface change. Do the highest-$ AZ page last, after the pattern is proven on home/CO.
- **Risk:** YMM dead-ends to 'call for accurate pricing' — the lowest-friction tab — making any price placement strictly worse than today's /book path for users without plate/VIN handy.
  - **Mitigation:** Ship the YMM->plate/VIN nudge and plate-default tab in Phase 1 as a hard gate before ANY new price placement. No surface that can receive a YMM attempt launches against a dead-end.
- **Risk:** Internal price links carrying utm_* overwrite the original ad gclid/utm and reattribute paid conversions to self-referral; the /arizona hub already hardcodes utm_source=phoenix-landing (live bug).
  - **Mitigation:** Internal CTAs carry only ref= + prefill, never utm_*. Verify gclid/msclkid persist from ad landing through internal navigation to the conversion (?gclid=TEST walkthrough). Fix the /arizona utm hardcode in the same pass after confirming it is AZ-specific.
- **Risk:** Pathname-derived /book prefill mislabels per-city attribution (used for geo bidding) if the slug extractor mis-fires across the four route shapes, especially the /colorado/{city} bare-city -> {city}-co synthesis.
  - **Mitigation:** Slug helper returns null for non-city paths (emit bare /book). Ship unit tests for one route per shape + a non-city path, stamp ref=deeplink_city so a bad slug is detectable, and canary on a small city set verified in GA4 before full rollout.
- **Risk:** Embedding the heavy client quoter on server-rendered SEO content pages pays full LCP/CLS hydration cost (zero SEO benefit on a noindex paid LP) and needs net-new lazy infra that doesn't exist (next/dynamic used 0 times).
  - **Mitigation:** No embeds on the long tail or noindex paid LP — use deep-links/CTA repoints. Limit embeds to /pricing only, below the static body, behind a tested lazy wrapper validated by a mobile Lighthouse check first.
- **Risk:** Retiring InsuranceQuoteForm/QuoteForm/QuickCaptureForm (->/api/lead) to 'consolidate' would silently drop a live lead-capture conversion; /does-insurance-cover does NOT even import that form, so the survey's migration framing is wrong for it.
  - **Mitigation:** Move ALL legacy-form retirement and /api/lead changes OUT of this engagement into a separate council-approved workstream. In-scope insurance work is limited to the additive secondary cash-price link. Never remove a /api/lead endpoint without mapping downstream consumers.
- **Risk:** Harness IO drop this session prevented re-reading next.config.js, StickyMobileCTA.tsx, tracking.ts and the twin routes — reviewers disagree on the /quote redirect and the exact twin count.
  - **Mitigation:** Treat these as hard pre-code verification gates (below). Point the Header at / if a /quote redirect is confirmed, else /quote; never ADD a redirect. Confirm StickyMobileCTA's real /book link and the twin count by direct read in a working session before editing.

## Measurement plan
- YMM demand: count quote_attempt_<tab> (plate vs VIN vs YMM) by surface and market, so the share of users choosing the un-priceable YMM tab is visible — the data that justifies or kills a NAGS license.
- Nudge effectiveness: track quote_ymm_miss -> quote_nudge_replate -> subsequent quote_form(priced) to measure what fraction of dead-ended YMM attempts recover into a live price (and grow the VIN->NAGS cache).
- Per-surface conversion: require {surface} on every quote_form so priced-rate and book-through are comparable across home, /quote, /pricing, AZ landers, and Header-driven traffic.
- Per-market conversion: require {market: CO|AZ} on quote_form and booking_form (resolved from resolveMarket or session first-touch on agnostic shells) so CO-vs-AZ conversion rate stays measurable after the quoter reaches market-agnostic pages — foundational for cross-market budget splits.
- Ad-tracking integrity: confirm quote_form AND booking_form are counted goals in both Google and Microsoft Ads before each paid change; annotate change dates; verify exactly one conversion per completed job and that gclid/msclkid persist end-to-end via a ?gclid=TEST walkthrough.
- Long-tail prefill health: stamp ref=deeplink_city on prefilled bookings and assert in GA4 (canary city set) that booking_form carries the correct city before full rollout; treat a city mismatch as a slug-bug anomaly.
- Page-speed guardrail: for any embed (Phase 4 /pricing), a before/after mobile Lighthouse delta (LCP/CLS/TBT) confirming the LCP element stays server-rendered copy and CLS stays ~0.
- Blast-radius watch: monitor booking_form completion volume (not just quote_form fires) around the Header link + prefill rollout to ensure the working booking path is not depressed.
