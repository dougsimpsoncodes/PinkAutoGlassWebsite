## 1. LOCAL FALCON — Full Profile

**Website:** https://www.localfalcon.com
**Tagline:** "Track + Optimize Local & AI Search Visibility"
**Positioning:** Industry's first local-focused Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) platform

---

### 1.1 Full Feature List

#### A. Geo-Grid Rank Tracking (Core Feature)
- Visual heatmap of Google Maps/Local Pack rankings across a geographic grid
- Grid sizes: **3x3, 5x5, 7x7, 9x9, 11x11, 13x13, 15x15, 17x17, 19x19, 21x21** (largest in the category)
- Ability to **disable specific grid points** (e.g., ocean, mountains, irrelevant areas)
- Scan by keyword and location center
- GPS-spoofed scanning for precise location simulation
- Service Area Business (SAB) support with wider radius options
- Mobile and desktop search simulation
- Tracks ranking positions at each individual map pin / grid node
- Neighborhood-level heatmaps — color-coded green/yellow/red by rank position

#### B. AI Search Visibility Tracking (Unique, industry-first)
- Tracks business visibility inside **ChatGPT** responses
- Tracks **Google AI Overviews** (SGE)
- Tracks **Google Gemini**
- Tracks **Google AI Mode**
- Tracks **Grok** (xAI)
- Uses same geo-grid visualization format for AI platforms as for Google Maps
- **Share of AI Voice (SAIV)** metric — proprietary, measures AI search presence
- AI scan report analysis available for all AI platforms

#### C. Apple Maps Tracking
- Full geo-grid rank tracking for **Apple Business Connect** / Apple Maps
- Scan Report AI Analysis available for Apple Maps scans
- Critical for US iPhone user coverage

#### D. Key Metrics
- **SoLV (Share of Local Voice):** % of grid points where business appears in the top-3 Local Pack
- **ARP (Average Rank Position):** average ranking across all grid points where business appeared
- **ATRP (Average Total Rank Position):** average rank including points where business did NOT appear (gets a rank of 20)
- **SAIV (Share of AI Voice):** same concept as SoLV but for AI platforms

#### E. Competitor Tracking
- Every scan shows ALL competitors that appeared in the Local Pack anywhere on the grid
- Per-competitor data: ARP, ATRP, SoLV scores, address, review score, business categories
- Shows how many grid points each competitor appeared in
- Competitor overlay on the same heatmap
- Market share comparison across competitors

#### F. Campaign Automation & Scheduling
- **Campaigns:** automated recurring scans on a schedule
- Set scan frequency per keyword/location
- Campaign-level reporting across multiple locations and keywords
- Automated reporting delivery

#### G. Falcon AI (AI-Powered Analysis)
- Generates AI analysis and optimization recommendations after each scan
- Costs **25 credits per AI Analysis Report**
- Included free with all annual and enterprise plans
- Available for Google Maps, Apple Maps, and all AI platform scans
- Analyzes ranking patterns, identifies opportunities, produces actionable insights

#### H. Falcon Agent (Agentic AI)
- Autonomous AI tool for **bulk Google Business Profile optimization**
- Performs multi-step GBP improvements automatically
- Available as an agentic workflow tool

#### I. Falcon Guard (GBP Monitoring)
- Monitors Google Business Profile for changes **twice daily**
- Alerts on: address pin moved, description changes, review count/score changes, category changes, any listing edit
- Protection against: competitor black-hat edits, user-suggested edits, accidental changes
- Also tracks **GBP Performance Metrics**: calls, website clicks (visits), direction requests
- Stores metrics forever (long-term trend visibility)
- Reports: share via direct link, PDF, or CSV
- Downloadable as SVG, PNG, or CSV
- **Pricing: $0.10 per location per month** (add-on, not in base plan)

#### J. Sales Enablement Tool
- Upload a CSV of sales leads (business names)
- Tool auto-populates each lead's ARP, ATRP, and SoLV from their Google Business Profile
- Enables personalized outreach emails showing prospects their exact rankings
- Costs **1 credit per lead lookup**
- Available on Basic plan and above

#### K. Reporting
- **Scan Reports:** full grid view with heatmap, metrics, competitor list
- **Competitor Reports:** performance overview of every competitor found in a scan
- **Trend Reports:** historical ranking changes over time
- **Keyword Reports:** performance by keyword
- **Location Reports:** performance by location
- **Campaign Reports:** aggregate reporting across multiple locations/keywords
- **Falcon Guard Protection Reports:** GBP change log + performance metrics

- Export formats: **PNG, PDF, CSV, SVG**
- Shareable public links (view-only, no login required)
- White-label reporting (custom logo, colors, headers) — available on Premium plan
- **Looker Studio (Google Data Studio) integration** — available on Basic plan and above
  - Pre-built Looker Studio templates provided
  - Fully downloadable, editable, and shareable dashboards
  - White-labeled on Basic+ plans
- **Zapier integration** — available on Basic plan and above
  - Connect to 5,000+ apps with no code
  - Automate keyword, location, scan, and trend reports
  - Falcon Guard alerts can trigger Zapier workflows

#### L. API
- Full **Data Retrieval API** (free with Basic plan and above)
- Authentication: API key or OAuth 2.1
- **MCP Server** at https://mcp.localfalcon.com — integrates with any agentic AI system
- npm package: `@local-falcon/mcp`
- OpenAPI spec available at https://docs.localfalcon.com/openapi.yaml

#### M. Multi-Location & Team Features
- Track 30M+ locations globally
- Role-based access controls for teams
- Team management dashboard
- Supports franchises and multi-location businesses
- Track any business's location (not just your own — useful for audits and prospecting)

#### N. Other Notable Features
- **"Visualize any business" tool** — run a scan on any competitor without owning the GBP
- Global coverage (works in any country)
- 100 free promotional credits for new accounts
- Pay-as-you-go credit purchases available (never expire)

### 1.3 Target Customer
- **Primary:** Local SEO agencies and consultants managing multiple client GBPs
- **Secondary:** Multi-location franchise businesses

### 1.4 What Local Falcon Does NOT Have

- **No GBP posting or content scheduling** — cannot publish Google posts from within the tool
- **No review management** — cannot see, respond to, or solicit reviews
- **No citation building** — no NAP correction, no submission to directories
- **No photo management** — cannot upload/schedule photos to GBP
- **No Q&A management** — cannot manage GBP questions and answers
- **No listing locking** — Falcon Guard only alerts, does NOT auto-revert changes (Local Viking does auto-revert)
- **No Bing Places tracking** — Google and Apple Maps only (no Bing)
- **No organic rank tracking** — focused solely on local pack / map rankings, not organic blue links
- **No review generation** — no automated review request SMS/email to customers
- **No built-in CRM or lead management**
- **No call tracking**
- **No mobile app**
- **No phone support** — chat and email only
- **Credits expire** — monthly credits are use-it-or-lose-it (a real pain point for agencies)
- **Costs scale steeply** — large grids (21x21) at high frequency burn credits very fast
- **No report template variety** — fewer pre-designed layouts vs. BrightLocal
- **Looker Studio requires technical setup** for lower-tier users

## 2. LOCAL VIKING — Full Profile

**Website:** https://www.localviking.com
**Tagline:** Implied: "Google Business Profile Management + GeoGrid Rank Tracking"
**Positioning:** All-in-one GBP management platform for agencies and multi-location businesses

---

### 2.1 Full Feature List

#### A. GeoGrid Rank Tracking (Core Feature)
- Visual heatmap of Google Maps/Local Pack rankings using geospatial GPS coordinates
- Grid sizes: up to **13x13** (smaller maximum than Local Falcon's 21x21)
- Automatic scan scheduling with configurable intervals
- Historical archive of all scans
- **Timelapse GIFs** generated from historical scan data to show ranking improvement over time
- Live search capabilities
- Global coverage ("works GLOBALLY")
- Mobile and desktop search distinction
- Click-through on any grid node to see full competitor list at that point
- **GeoGrid Widget** — embeddable widget for client-facing dashboards (Pro plan+)

#### B. GeoGrid Metrics
- **AGR (Average Grid Rank):** average ranking across grid points where business appeared — lower is better
- **ATGR (Average Total Grid Rank):** average including non-appearing points — lower is better
- **SoLV (Share of Local Voice):** % of grid points appearing in top-3 Local Pack

#### C. Keyword Rank Tracking
- Traditional (non-grid) local keyword rank tracking
- Mobile vs. desktop distinction
- Historical trending

#### D. GBP Post Scheduling (Key differentiator vs. Local Falcon)
- Schedule **Standard, Offer, and Event** post types
- **Bulk CSV uploading** for mass post creation
- **Spintax support** — auto-generates variations across similar posts (e.g., {great|amazing|excellent} for content diversity)
- **Recurring posts** — set a post to repeat on a schedule indefinitely
- **Post daisy chaining** — chain posts so one starts when another ends
- **Calendar view** for managing scheduled content
- Multi-location posting — push one post to hundreds of locations simultaneously
- Post to all locations with one action

#### E. GBP Photo Scheduling & EXIF Metadata Injection
- Schedule photo uploads to GBP
- **EXIF metadata injection** — embed keywords + GPS latitude/longitude coordinates into image metadata before upload
- Pulls existing GMB location data to pre-populate EXIF fields
- Bulk image uploading to multiple locations
- Claimed to boost local rankings via geotagged images
- Customer photo integration

#### F. Listing Management & Protection
- Centralized dashboard for all GBP locations
- **Attribute Sentry (Attribute Locking):** automatically detects and **auto-reverts** unauthorized edits to listing information
  - Protects: business name, address, phone, categories, attributes, description
  - Sends notifications when changes are detected and reverted
- Suspension notifications — alerts when a location gets suspended
- Edit location information directly from the dashboard
- Bulk actions: mass enable/disable/add/remove tags across hundreds of locations
- Sort and search optimized for accounts managing thousands of locations

#### G. Review Management
- Centralized review dashboard for all locations
- Review notifications via phone/email
- **AI-generated review replies** — matches brand tone, with customizable settings (tone, formality, personality traits)
- Allows manual review and edit of AI suggestions before posting
- Q&A and FAQ management — bulk upload questions and answers to GBP
- **No built-in review generation/solicitation** (requires separate integration with Rep.co)

#### H. GBP Audit Reports
- In-depth analysis of your GBP vs. competitors
- AI analyst commentary on your location's strengths and weaknesses vs. competition
- Section-by-section competitive commentary

#### I. GeoBooster (Mobile App — Add-on, $99/location/month)
- Native iPhone and Android app
- Enables employees to create **hyperlocal content ("Moments")** from job sites
- Moments = short descriptions + photos (before/after) created at job location
- Content tagged by category and geography
- Integrates with a custom WordPress plugin for **dynamic page creation** based on content tags
- Geo-tagged content (GPS coordinates embedded)
- Syndication engine: content flows from app → WordPress pages → GBP posts
- Mixed reviews on ranking impact (community debate on effectiveness)

#### J. Reporting & White-Label
- White-label reporting (available on Pro plan, $99/mo and above)
- Customizable client reports
- Automated report scheduling and delivery
- **Google Data Studio connector** — feed Local Viking data into custom dashboards
- Export formats: CSV, timelapse GIFs, PNG (PNG listed as "upcoming")
- Shareable report links

#### K. Integrations
- **Google Data Studio** connector
- **Zapier** — CRM, POS, and booking tool integration via Zapier
- **Rep.co** — for review generation/solicitation (external, not native)
- Call tracking tool (new, integrated into dashboard)
- **Form tracking tool** (listed on roadmap, Q1-Q2 2026)

#### L. Upcoming/Roadmap Features (as of early 2026)
- GBP Post AI Assistant
- GBP Audit AI annotations
- One-time credit purchases for keywords and GeoGrid
- Form tracking tool

#### M. Multi-Location & Team Features
- Manage hundreds to thousands of GBP locations from one dashboard
- Unlimited Google accounts connected (Starter plan and above)
- Team management
- Tags and labels for location organization
- Bulk operations across all locations

## 4. MARKET GAPS & OPPORTUNITIES

Based on this research, here is what **both tools lack** — these are the clearest opportunities for a competing product:

### Gap 1: Unified Platform (Tracking + Management + AI)
Neither tool does everything. Local Falcon is tracking-only. Local Viking does management but lacks AI search tracking. A tool that combines geo-grid tracking, GBP posting, review management, AND AI search visibility in one would be category-defining.

### Gap 2: Bing Places / Microsoft Tracking
Neither tool tracks Bing Places / Microsoft Maps rankings at all. With Microsoft integrating AI heavily into Bing, this is a growing blind spot.

### Gap 3: Citation / NAP Management
Both tools completely punt on citation building and NAP consistency. Users must subscribe to a separate tool (Moz Local, Yext, BrightLocal Citations, etc.) and manage two separate systems.

### Gap 4: Review Generation (Native)
Both tools lack a native "request a review" feature. Local Viking has AI reply but no solicitation. A tool with automated post-service review request SMS/email would eliminate another vendor dependency.

### Gap 5: Predictable Pricing Without Credit Anxiety
The credit-based model (Local Falcon) and dual-credit-pool model (Local Viking) both create "credit anxiety" for agencies — rationing scans instead of maximizing client value. A flat-rate model (unlimited scans within plan) could be a major differentiator.

### Gap 6: AI-Powered Ranking Explanations
Local Falcon gives AI recommendations but they are per-scan add-ons. Local Viking gives raw data with no interpretation. A tool that automatically explains WHY rankings changed (and what to do) after every scan — without extra credits — would stand out.

### Gap 7: No True Small Business / DIY Tier
Both tools are built for agencies. There is no simple, affordable, self-serve option for a single-location small business owner. Local Viking's cheapest plan ($39/mo) is still credit-based and confusing. An "easy mode" for non-technical local business owners is unserved.

### Gap 8: No Organic + Local Unified View
Neither tracks both organic keyword rankings AND local pack rankings in the same dashboard. Agencies must use a separate tool (Semrush, Ahrefs, Moz) for organic — another vendor.

### Gap 9: No Real-Time Alerts
Both tools are batch/scheduled. There is no real-time notification when your ranking drops significantly (e.g., "You fell out of the 3-Pack for 'windshield replacement Phoenix' — 48 hours ago"). Near-real-time monitoring is an unserved need.

### Gap 10: GeoBooster Equivalent Without the Cost
Local Viking's GeoBooster concept (employee creates job-site content from mobile app → syndicates to GBP + website) is powerful but costs $99/location/month on top of the base plan. A built-in, lower-cost version of this would be very attractive to service businesses.
