Competitive Strategy
Where we're behind, where we're ahead, and what to do about it

Where Competitors Are Ahead

  • Google Local Services Ads. Competitors run LSAs at $6–30/lead — appears above regular ads. Pink isn't on LSA yet.
  • Review volume. Tavos: 1,324 reviews. Precision: 405. Volume dominates Maps even at lower ratings.
  • SEO / Domain authority. Top organic results have 5–20+ years of backlinks. Pink is 4 months old.

Where Pink Is Ahead

  • Customer sentiment remains strong in verbatims. Public profile snapshot currently shows a 4.5 rating.
  • Google Ads efficiency is stronger than Microsoft. Current CPA is $67 on Google vs. $143 on Microsoft.
  • Mobile-first "we come to you." Strongest value prop in the market. Many competitors still require shop visits.

Action Plan

Quick Wins (This Month)
  • Launch Google Local Services Ads — benchmark $6–30/lead vs. current blended paid CPA of $76
  • Push reviews past 100 — automated requests are live, need consistent completion flow
Medium Term (1–3 Months)
  • Optimize Microsoft CPC from $14.13 toward single-digit CPC while maintaining lead quality
  • Add ADAS calibration messaging — 80%+ of new vehicles need it
Long Game (3–12 Months)
  • Build organic rankings — target page 1 for top city terms
  • Grow backlinks through location pages, content, and local partnerships
Opportunities & Action Items
What to fix, improve, and prioritize next

Data Hygiene Issues

  • Omega invoice dates not being set — we can't accurately track when jobs are completed vs. when they're paid. Need consistent date entry.
  • Lead statuses not updated — most Supabase leads still show "new" status. Need to mark leads as contacted/quoted/scheduled/completed to measure real conversion rates.
  • No phone numbers in Omega — can't match Omega jobs back to website leads. Adding phone to Omega invoices would enable end-to-end attribution.
  • Tracking baseline is established. Supabase currently shows 8,543 sessions and 7,645 unique visitors for Oct 1–Feb 11.

In Progress

  • Beetexting SMS migration — RingCentral programmatic SMS doesn't show in Beetexting UI. Dan needs to create Beetexting-native credentials to complete the integration.
  • Google Business Profile — review widget and map embed built, deployed on Denver page. 43 other location pages still need updating.

Growth Opportunities

  • Rebalance paid budget toward better CPA channels. Google is currently more efficient than Microsoft ($67 vs. $143 CPA); adjust allocation with lead-quality checks.
  • Google Reviews — automated review request system is live. Every completed job triggers SMS + email asking for a review. Need to monitor and grow review count.
  • SEO / Organic — GSC shows 9,563 impressions, 45 clicks, and avg position 38.7 in the current window. Improving rank and snippets should lift CTR from 0.47%.
  • Seasonal planning — Dec was peak ($15.6K). Spring hail season could be bigger. Prepare ad budget and staffing.
