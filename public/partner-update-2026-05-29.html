What the auto-quoter actually does

Four steps. No phone call required. Customer can complete on iPhone in under a minute.

  1. 1
    Identify the vehicle

    License plate (default) + state, OR VIN, OR Year/Make/Model dropdowns. Plate lookup decodes the VIN automatically via AutoBolt's plate-to-VIN API.

  2. 2
    Look up the glass

    Mygrant API returns the exact NAGS part for the vehicle + trim. Filters out incompatible parts (no HUD glass for non-HUD trims, etc.). Confirms inventory and delivery ETA at our branch.

  3. 3
    Calculate the price

    Pricing engine applies our locked markup rules (see below). Customer sees a real installed price in seconds. $299 minimum floor on every quote.

  4. 4
    Book the install

    Inline form: name + phone + address + ZIP + a time pill (Tomorrow AM / Tomorrow PM / next working day AM/PM). Customer confirmation and team alert (SMS + email) fire automatically. ZIP-to-city handled server-side.

Customer message
"Get an instant quote and book your install. No phone calls, no callbacks, real price in seconds."

What fires after booking

Four messages go out the second the customer hits "Book this install." Two to them, two to your team. None of them slow down the "You're booked!" screen, so if email or SMS hiccups, the booking is still safe in the database.

What we gain, what we trade off

✓ Gains
  • Conversion lift on mobile. Price-first beats info-first for the 2.4× higher mobile cohort.
  • ~40% revenue lift per closed quote on average vs current manual pricing (see examples).
  • Removes the phone call from price discovery. Customers who don't want to talk get served.
  • Consistent pricing. Same vehicle, same answer, no human discretion variance.
  • Tech notifications include vehicle data + glass + cost + margin. Better install-day prep.
  • ADAS tiered handling. Addresses the 55% decline pattern without losing the safety-critical work.
⚠ Trade-offs
  • Luxury impact varies by ADAS type. Tier 1/2 markup dropped from $300/$400 to $100/$200. Combined with ADAS now bundled only when mandatory (Static cal), BMW base trim (Dynamic) is +23%, Lexus Safety+ (Static) is +76%. Wider spread.
  • ~10% of jobs route to manual review: commercial vans, light trucks with unusual glass, exotics. Still need phone quotes for those.
  • Insurance flow unchanged. Auto-quoter is cash-only. Insurance bookings still come in via the legacy form / phone.
  • Less human judgment on edge cases. If the engine flags ADAS on a vehicle that really shouldn't need it, the quote inflates.
  • Mygrant + AutoBolt dependency. When those APIs are down, the quote tool routes to a phone CTA.
